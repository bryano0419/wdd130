Home page

General Over View KSB services is a small family owned home improvement and repair company specializing in repairing, maintaining, and improving residential properties. This company offers a variety of services, including plumbing repairs, electrical work, HVAC repairs, roofing, painting, carpentry, and more.. Our main priority is customer satisfaction, not only do we offer quality work but we work to build a trust based relationship with all our customers so that with just one phone call anything you need for your home, it will never be an issue again. And with a variety of services ranging from small handyman jobs to adding new sections to your house, paired with reasonable and flexible prices we strive to become your first choice for anything home related.

More information

History:

KSB Services starting as a family owned buissness has been around for decades and have evolved over time to meet the changing needs of homeowners. The companys founder started the company with nothing to his name and no knowledge in the field but due to difficult cirumstances he threw himself in and began as a worker learning about everything he could and using that knwoledge he began to grow his buissness he started hiring workers and expanding his area of service reling on word-of-mouth referrals and local advertising to attract customers. Soon it began to expand its services offered adding specialized specific areas of home repair, such as roofing or HVAC systems.

Jobs include but are not limited to:

Jobs include but are not limited to: Plumbing repairs, including fixing leaky faucets, unclogging drains, and repairing or replacing pipes. Electrical repairs, including fixing outlets, switches, and light fixtures, as well as installing new electrical panels or circuits. HVAC repairs, including maintaining and repairing heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems. Roof repairs, including fixing leaks, replacing shingles, and repairing damage caused by storms or other weather events. Window and door repairs, including fixing broken glass, replacing damaged frames, and repairing or replacing hardware. Flooring repairs, including fixing cracks, replacing damaged tiles or planks, and refinishing hardwood floors. Painting and drywall repairs, including fixing holes, cracks, and other damage to walls and ceilings. Fence and deck repairs, including fixing broken boards, replacing damaged posts, and refinishing or staining wood surfaces. Gutter and downspout repairs, including fixing leaks, replacing damaged sections, and cleaning out debris. General handyman services, including minor repairs and maintenance tasks such as hanging shelves, installing light fixtures, and assembling furniture.
